Don't just use this button. It is here if the machine stopped working, e.g. if one of the axis failed or someone pressed the Emergency Stop. In those cases, brain alive should be false. Before reinitializing, please make sure that the big axis is aligned again (as explained) and the Emergency Stop is not active.
